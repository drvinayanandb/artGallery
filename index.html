I have been a homemaker for nearly thirty five years now and I have always felt an inclination towards expressing myself creatively. From painting on regular surfaces such as canvas and paper, to finding ordinary items of everyday use to sweep my brush across, I have tried to bring a burst of colour to my home at every opportunity. During lockdown, I got the opportunity to take an online art course and it really opened my eyes to the plethora of opportunities before me. I was able to finetune my skills and venture out of my comfort zone to experiment with colour palettes, brushes, surfaces and textures.

Please browse through this website and let me know which of my creations you loved, and which require more effort. I'll be happy to hear from you!
